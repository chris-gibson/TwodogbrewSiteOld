If you found this page, you probably received (and are mighty brave if you consumed) a bottle of my first attempt at brewing beer.

I commissioned Julie Kiefer at Julie A. Kiefer Design (who is widely known from her design on the Shedteau Project) to design the label. Since it's a Red Ale, I thought it was appropriate to include our red haired dog Daisy on the label. Since she and Buck (Buckamus Rex) are inseparable, he found his way onto the label too.

You'll want to keep the bottle, not because it's going to be a collectors item someday, but because when you return it to me, I'll give you a full one in return. Don't worry with pulling the label, I can do that, just rinse and return.
